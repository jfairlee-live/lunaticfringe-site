About

Lunatic Fringe is an acoustic duo breathing new life into your favorite rock and country hits from the ‘70s through the early 2000s. With rich harmonies, tight rhythms, and a stripped-down style, we bring everything from classic rock ballads to boot-stomping country anthems to life.
